About Cult

An experimental tooling company.

We build tools that assume the future is uncertain and that people are more interesting than their profiles.

Most software optimizes for the obvious: more engagement, more retention, more data. We’re interested in the edges—the layover, the cancelled train, the line you didn’t want to be in, the route you’ve taken for years without looking up. Those are the places where something unexpected can happen.

Cult is not a brand in the usual sense. It’s a label for experiments. Up In The Air is the first: a dating app that matches you to people on your way, from your itinerary alone. No location tracking. No “same flight” in the feed—just “on the way.” You decide if and when to reveal more. We think that’s safer and weirder and more human.

We care about privacy, safety, and technical clarity. We’d rather explain exactly how something works than hide behind “the algorithm.” We’d rather build with tools that let us ship to web and native without pretending the web is second-class. We’re small, opinionated, and okay with being wrong in public.

If that sounds like your kind of experiment, we’re on the right track. Stay in touch.
